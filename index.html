Andresito Escobar

The Gentle Defender

Here is a time line of Andres Escobar's life and career:

  • 13 March 1967- Born in Medellin, Colombia.

  • Escobar was a defender throughout his career and he wore the no.2 jersey.

  • He was known by the nickname El Caballero del Futbol (The Gentleman of Football).

  • 1989- He won the Copa Libertodores with Atletico Nacional.

  • He played for only 2 clubs in his career, Atletico Nacional and Young Boys.

  • 30 March 1988- He made his debut for the Colombia national team.

  • 1988- He made his competitive debut for Colombia.

  • 1988- He scored the only goal of his career for his country in a 1-1 vs England.

  • 1989- Escobar got engaged to his girlfriend Pamela

  • 1990- He played in his first world cup, Italia '90

  • 22 June 1994- He scored an own-goal in a world game against the USA.

  • 2 July 1994- Andres was killed at the parking lot of El Indio night club as a result of the own-goal.

  • 3 July 1994- Andres was buried with over 120,000 people in attendance.

"Andres Escobar always in the hearts of all of us. We will never forget your Kindness, humility and struggle. I miss you brother I miss you"

-- Ex-colombia captain, Carlos Valderrama
